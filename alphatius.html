Alphatius

Alphatius is a free-to-play multiplayer game that uses a heavily modifed version of the classic "Doom Engine". Using the engine known as Q-Zandronum, Alphatius creates a unique Arena Shooter experience inspired by a blend of modern games like Call of Duty and Halo, and old-school shooters like Quake 3 Arena and Unreal Tournament. Featuring new weapons, new mechanics, and nearly 20 built-in maps, it's aim is to be fast-paced, action-packed, and fun!

Download Alphatius (ver. 006b)

Content and Features

  • 7 Weapons, each with its own Alternate Fire mode, found throughout the maps.
  • A unique loadout system that allows players choose their starting weapon and their abilities.
  • 5 Travel abilities, allowing players to quickly evade attacks, or traverse the map more quickly.
  • 6 Action abilities to aid in combat. These range from decoys, bouncing energy discs, healing zones, forcefields, or even laser cannons!
  • 6 Passive abilities that help encourage players to pursue their unique playstyles.
  • 29 different Weapon Modifiers, that allow players to swap the functionality of their weapons.
  • 19 unique maps; 13 deathmatch maps for fraggy fun, and 6 duel maps for some more serious competition.
  • A unique ammo system: Weapon energy recharges after a few seconds of "trigger discipline".
  • 4 special pickups to aid in battle, most of which revolve around the new mechanics.
  • A built-in tutorial to teach new players how to play, and an additional Firing Range map to test out weapons and abilities.
