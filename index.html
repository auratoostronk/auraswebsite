ALPHATIUS

A deathmatch mod for DOOM II:


This mod started out as a neat idea inspired by Seidolon's DOOM ULTIMATE LASER ARENA, or "DULA" for short. Contrary to the chaotic atmosphere of DULA, ALPHATIUS was designed with balance in mind, while also keeping the fun, fast-paced and action-packed gameplay of DULA and DOOM II. Currently there are 6 different weapons with two fire modes each, 6 exclusive maps, 4 travel abilities that allow the player to maneuver around terrain, and multiple weapon mods that alter the behavior of the weapons. These gameplay elements combine to make a deathmatch mod like no other! Requires Zandronum.

AURA'S INSTAGIB CHAOS

All it takes is one hit!


Ever wondered what Deathmatch would be if every weapon killed you in one hit? Insanely imbalanced, right? Well, what if we took that concept, and somehow made it fair? Presenting AURA'S INSTAGIB CHAOS! A mod where anything and everything can and will kill you if it hits you! One might say, "How can this be balanced at all?" To which I would respond, "If you think this mod uses hitscans, then you need to think a little harder." "Projectiles!" is your answer! Some are fast and short ranged, some are slow but travel indefinitely. Some even seek after enemies! Some take time to charge up! Overall, its a blast; something you need to see for yourself in action! Requires Zandronum.

BRADLEY'S MOD

A parody of Minecraft mods as a Minecraft mod?



This mod is a joke mod for minecraft. The story behind it involves a fat minecraft addict named Bradley who wanted to make a mod but didn't know how to code, so he hired someone to code the mod for him. However, since Bradley is an imbecile, his mod consists of terrible gameplay and bland concepts. The mod parodies other minecraft mods with unoriginal ideas (i.e. the classic "obsidian tools"), while being intentionally terrible at the same time, but somehow manages to be crazy and fun! Requires Minecraft Forge 1.12.2
