AURA'S INSTAGIB CHAOS

All it takes is one hit!

Ever wondered what Deathmatch would be if every weapon killed you in one hit? Insanely imbalanced, right? Well, what if we took that concept, and somehow made it fair? Presenting AURA'S INSTAGIB CHAOS! A mod where anything and everything can and will kill you if it hits you! One might say, "How can this be balanced at all?" To which I would respond, "If you think this mod uses hitscans, then you need to think a little harder." "Projectiles!" is your answer! Some are fast and short ranged, some are slow but travel indefinitely. Some even seek after enemies! Some take time to charge up! Overall, its a blast; something you need to see for yourself in action! Requires Zandronum.
