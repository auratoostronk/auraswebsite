Alphatius

Alphatius is a multiplayer mod that uses a heavily modifed version of the classic "Doom Engine" (idTech 1). Using the engine known as Q-Zandronum, Alphatius creates a unique Arena Shooter experience designed for casual and competitive gamers alike. Featuring new weapons, new mechanics, and over 18 built-in maps, it's aim is to be fair, balanced, fast-paced and fun.

Aura CTF

AuraCTF is a map pack for DOOM II: Hell on Earth's Capture the Flag gamemode added by the source port Zandronum. The map set is designed to be fast paced and action packed, while maintaining a level of balance for competitive play. Many maps bring ideas to the CTF scene that have never been seen before!
