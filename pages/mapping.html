AuraCTF

AuraCTF is a work-in-progress map pack for DOOM II: Hell on Earth's Capture the Flag gamemode. The map set is designed to be fast paced and action packed, while maintaining a level of balance for competitive play. Many maps bring ideas to the CTF scene that have never been seen before!
