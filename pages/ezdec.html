EZDec

EZDec is an application that makes Doom modding easy. It allows users to create new weapons for zdoom-based ports with an easy-to-use GUI.

How to use

Once you have customized your weapon, click "Create" to generate a .pk3 file. This file can be dragged and dropped onto GZDoom or Zandronum to load your weapon into the game. From there, you can give the weapon to yourself through console commands. Future releases will allow users to replace existing weapons in the game with their own!
