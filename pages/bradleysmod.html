BRADLEY'S MOD

A parody of Minecraft mods as a Minecraft mod?

This mod is a joke mod for minecraft. The story behind it involves a fat minecraft addict named Bradley who wanted to make a mod but didn't know how to code, so he hired someone to code the mod for him. However, since Bradley is an imbecile, his mod consists of terrible gameplay and bland concepts. The mod parodies other minecraft mods with unoriginal ideas (i.e. the classic "obsidian tools"), while being intentionally terrible at the same time, but somehow manages to be crazy and fun! Requires Minecraft Forge 1.12.2
